Head Coach: Sarah Anderson Background: Sarah Anderson, born on March 15, 1975, is a former professional hockey player turned successful coach.

Coaching Career: Sarah Anderson started her coaching career as an assistant coach with a prominent university hockey team, where she played a key role in recruiting and developing young talents. Her success at the university level led to her appointment as the head coach of a professional women's hockey team, where she made history by guiding the team to its first-ever championship in the inaugural season under her leadership. Sarah's coaching philosophy centers around a strong work ethic, strategic gameplay, and fostering a positive team culture. Her ability to connect with players, coupled with her tactical acumen, has earned her widespread respect in the hockey community.
